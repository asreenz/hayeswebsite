Hayes Dunlap is a writer and sometimes poet out of Austin, Texas with plans to be in New York City for the summer of 2021.    

He also films and edits video projects as a personal creative outlet.

Hayes likes drinking water, astrology, and holding hands with people.

Hayes dislikes coconut milk, the color purple, and people who do not recognize the transformative power of love.
